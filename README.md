## Remarks

- The ratio **does not necessarily reflect the video**, there are videos that were uploaded with an AR but it is actually cropped and the actual image has another AR
- Integrating SEO (Search engine optimization) strategies can significantly increase your videos' visibility. Focus on:
  - **Keywords and Hashtags:** These are vital in signalling the algorithm about your content's relevancy.
  - **Video Descriptions and Captions:** Utilise these to convey your message succinctly and include relevant keywords.
  - **Engagement and Interactions:** Encourage viewers to interact with your content, as this boosts algorithmic visibility.
  - **Consistency and Frequency:** Regular posting keeps your audience engaged and signals the algorithm of your active presence.
  - **Collaborations and Duets:** These can expand your reach to new audiences and increase engagement.
